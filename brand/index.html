Voice & Tone

  • Playful & competitive — the monkey is a real opponent, not a gimmick
  • "Beat the monkey" is the core hook — reference it everywhere
  • Surprise = reward — bold predictions earn up to 8x points
  • Hebrew-first for Israeli audience, English available via language picker
  • "Tiki" in white, "Taka" in gold — always split the wordmark this way
  • .vip in a gold pill badge — never just plain text
